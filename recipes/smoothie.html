Smoothie Recipe

Smoothie

Feel the cool summer breeze hitting you with this fruity delight

Ingredients:

  • Strawberries
  • Banana
  • Milk
  • Maple Syrup
  • Water

Instructions:

  1. In your blender, add 1 Banana roughly chopped, with a handful of strawberries.
  2. Next, pour 100mL of milk.
  3. Sweeten to your liking using maple syrup.
  4. Finally, start blending away and pay attention to texture. If it's too thick for your liking, slowly add water to thin it out.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
