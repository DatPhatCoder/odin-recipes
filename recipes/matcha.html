Matcha Recipe

Matcha Latte

The best morning drink filled with antioxidants and energizing nutrients

Watch as the caffeine and theanine roll into your system and wake you up, without the crash of coffee

Ingredients:

  • Ceremonial/Culinary Grade Matcha Tea
  • Oat Milk
  • Water
  • Maple Syrup

Instructions:

  1. You want to start by sifting 1 or 2 teaspoons of Matcha into an empty bowl.
  2. Next, pour 10mL of 75 degree celsius water into the bowl and using a tool of your liking, rid of clumps, and smoothen it out.
  3. Sweeten to your liking using maple syrup and continue to mix.
  4. Pour ice cubes into an empty cup, 3/4 of the way up and pour 200mL of Oat Milk.
  5. Finally, pour the Matcha concoction you just made into your glass and leave it for 1 minute to dissolve. And then enjoy!
